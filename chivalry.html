Hunting

A favorite passtime of knights is hunting. This helps to develop the skills and traits of a knight.

Tournaments

Knights can engage in tournaments. This is another way for a knight to develop the skills and traits.

Settlers

Settlers of the gentry class are suitable for the recruitment of cavalry units and primarily work on creating armies.
